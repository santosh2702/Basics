Main Uses of NLTK

Text Preprocessing

Tokenization (splitting text into words/sentences)

Removing stopwords ("is", "the", etc.)

Stemming and lemmatization (reducing words to root form)

Part-of-speech tagging (noun, verb, etc.)

Text Analysis

Named entity recognition (finding names, places, dates)

Frequency distribution (finding most common words)

Concordance (finding words in context)

Linguistic Data Access

Comes with many corpora (collections of text) for training & testing

Example: WordNet (word meanings, synonyms, antonyms)

Building NLP Applications

Chatbots

Sentiment analysis

Text classification (spam detection, topic classification)
